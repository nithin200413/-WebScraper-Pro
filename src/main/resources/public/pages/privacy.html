Privacy Policy

Last updated · June 2026

What we collect

We store only the information needed to run your account: your name, email address and a securely hashed password. Passwords are hashed with BCrypt and are never stored in plain text.

Scraped data

Data you extract is processed on the server to build your results and returned to your browser. Exports (JSON, CSV, PDF, etc.) are generated locally in your browser.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you signed in. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Data retention

Your account data is kept until you request deletion. This is a demo project, so do not store sensitive personal information.

Your rights

  • Request a copy of your account data.
  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Update your details at any time.

For privacy requests, see Contact.
